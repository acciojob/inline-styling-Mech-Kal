Flowers photos

Flowers are the reproductive structures found in flowering plants (angiosperms). They often display vibrant colors and pleasant scents to attract pollinators like insects and birds. They are essential for producing seeds and fruits, which allow the plant species to propagate. [Image of a variety of colorful flowers]

Roses pictures

Roses are woody perennial flowering plants of the genus Rosa, known for their beauty and fragrance. There are over three hundred species and thousands of cultivars. They come in various colors, each with its own symbolic meaning, such as red for love and yellow for friendship.
